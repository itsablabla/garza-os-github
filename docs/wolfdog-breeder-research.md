# Wolfdog Breeder and Source Research

Research date: 2026-07-05

This is a lead list, not an endorsement list. Wolfdog ownership and transport are restricted or banned in many states, counties, cities, and countries. Before contacting any breeder, verify legality with state wildlife rules, county/city animal control, landlord/insurance rules, and a veterinarian who is willing to treat wolfdogs. Do not rely on a breeder's legality claims.

## Buyer Guardrails

- Prefer adoption/rescue evaluation before purchase. Wolfdog rescues report frequent relinquishment from underprepared buyers.
- Verify exact wolf content with recent Embark or equivalent DNA results for the parents, not percentage claims alone.
- Ask for OFA/PennHIP or breed-appropriate orthopedic results, eye exams, genetic screening, vaccination/deworming records, and a written contract.
- Avoid sellers using urgency, shipping-only transactions, unverifiable "high content" claims, fake registries, or no screening of the buyer's fencing/experience.
- Require a lifetime return clause. Ethical breeders should take back animals they produced.
- For Czechoslovakian Vlciak / Czechoslovakian Wolfdog and Saarloos Wolfdog, distinguish recognized domestic breeds from recent wolf-dog hybrids. Import/export paperwork and breed legality can differ.

Useful due-diligence references:

- AVMA canine hybrid policy: https://www.avma.org/resources-tools/avma-policies/canine-hybrids
- Texas Wolfdog Project ownership warning: https://texaswolfdogproject.org/resources/owning-a-wolfdog
- Wolfdog Awareness responsible breeder checklist: https://www.wolfdogawareness.com/blog/responsible-wolfdog-breeder
- Czechoslovakian Vlciak Club of America breeder guidance: https://www.czechoslovakianvlciak.com/finding-a-csv
- Wolfdog.org Czechoslovakian Wolfdog breeder database: https://www.wolfdog.org/dbase/kennels

## Shortlist Recommendation

For a US buyer, start with the higher-signal official-site leads before directory-only listings:

1. Wolfdogs of the Ebon Blade
2. Southern Oregon Wolfdogs
3. Wander With Willow
4. SoCal Wolfdogs
5. Midnight Run Wolves
6. Winter Mountain Timber Dogs
7. DireWolf Pack or Guardian of the Carpathian if the goal is a Czechoslovakian Vlciak rather than a recent wolf-dog hybrid

For each, ask for:

- Parent Embark/DNA links and the expected content range.
- OFA/PennHIP/eye/genetic testing records for both parents.
- Photos/video of enclosures and puppy raising environment.
- Buyer screening requirements and fencing requirements.
- Contract terms, health guarantee, and lifetime return clause.
- Written confirmation that the breeder will not sell into illegal jurisdictions.

## Research Limitations

- Availability is seasonal and can change weekly.
- Some public breeder directories include inactive, private, or low-quality listings. Directory-only leads are marked as lower-confidence until direct contact confirms they are active and responsible; exact directory profile links or listing IDs are included where the directory exposes them.
- Wolfdog.org is valuable for Czechoslovakian Wolfdog breeder discovery, but it is a database/directory, not a guarantee of ethical practice.
- Private/internal datasets were not used for this public lead list.
